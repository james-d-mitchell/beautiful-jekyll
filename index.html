Counting monogenic semigroups

Oct 19, 2016
In this post we show how to count the number of monogenic subsemigroups of the full transformation monoid up to isomorphism. Throughout this post, we denote the natural numbers \(\{0, 1, \ldots\}\) by \({\mathbb{N}}\). The symmetric group and full transformation semigroup on \(\{1, \ldots, n\}\), \(n\in {\mathbb{N}}\), are denoted \(S_n\) and \(T_n\), respectively.
